Animationpub

Welcome to the site!

For anyone interested in animation and video games, you are in the right place.
This is the site dedicated to people who enjoy animation and video game.
This is a fan website and I merely showcase screenshots and gif that I collected through my time.
I envision that gif and images to be one of the best ways to communicate appreciation and support for a medium.
We are at the stage in which communications such as memes or blogs can be conveyed more efficiently through gifs.
So why not get that started? Why not create a visual discovery site that allows users to send and receive them?
