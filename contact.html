Breaking Bad

Who am I?

I am a second-year graduate student at the University of Michigan pursuing a Master of Science in Human-Computer Interaction. I believe that a good design should always understand users, care for users, and design for users.
I hope to use my design and technical skills to connect people of different background by bringing them together.

So why Breaking Bad?

As you have seen some of them, I like Breaking Bad. The universe that Vince Gilligan created is masterfully crafted with perfection. There never has been any other original television show that was able to produce a prequel and a sequel movie and all of them surpassing the predecessor. There will never be another television show like Breaking Bad and 100 years from now, people will look back and study Breaking Bad in school to understand filmmaking and culture of 2010s.

If you are a fan of the show like I am, you can contact me through my email by clicking here.
